FILM STORYBOARDS


Storyboarding is a unique yet simple way to see the whole picture of a project at once. You can use a Storyboards as a visual screenplay, at the writing stage of a film. You can use it to edit before filming: test how a story works in images, organise its sequences rhythm and flow. Storyboards are useful to plan, assess costs and ease communication between production crew members.

This blog will feature Storyboards, obviously, work in process, Sketches, doodles and pre production drawings as well as the occasional filmmaking references and links.
